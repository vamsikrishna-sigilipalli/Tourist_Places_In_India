About Manali

Manali, located in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, is a picturesque hill station known for its scenic beauty, lush green valleys, and snow-capped mountains. It is a popular destination for both adventure seekers and nature lovers. The town offers a variety of activities, including trekking, paragliding, river rafting, and skiing in the nearby Solang Valley. Rohtang Pass, located about 51 kilometers from Manali, is another major attraction that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and snow-filled landscapes, making it a must-visit for tourists. In addition to its natural beauty, Manali is rich in cultural heritage. The town is home to the Hadimba Temple, an ancient temple dedicated to the goddess Hadimba, known for its unique wooden architecture and tranquil surroundings. Old Manali, with its charming cafes and laid-back atmosphere, provides a contrast to the bustling town center. The hot springs at Vashisht Village and the beautiful Jogini Falls are also popular attractions, offering visitors a chance to relax and connect with nature.
