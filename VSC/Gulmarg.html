GULMARG

About Gulmarg

Gulmarg, located in the Pir Panjal range of Jammu & Kashmir, is renowned for its scenic beauty and year-round attractions. This picturesque town is famous for the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world, offering stunning views of the snow-clad Himalayan peaks. In winter, Gulmarg transforms into a skiing paradise, attracting adventurers from around the world to its powdery slopes. It also boasts the world’s highest golf course, making it a unique destination for golfers during the summer months. In addition to adventure sports, Gulmarg is known for its lush meadows, dense forests, and vibrant wildflowers that bloom in summer, making it a nature lover’s haven. The town's historical and religious sites, like the 14th-century Baba Reshi Shrine, add cultural depth to its natural beauty. Visitors can also enjoy trekking, horseback riding, and photography amidst the idyllic landscape. Gulmarg offers a perfect blend of relaxation and thrill, making it a top destination in Jammu & Kashmir.
