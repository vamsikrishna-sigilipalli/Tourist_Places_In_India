PAHALGAM

About Pahalgam

Pahalgam, a picturesque town nestled in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir, is renowned for its stunning landscapes and serene environment. Positioned at the confluence of the Lidder River and the Sheshnag Lake, it serves as a gateway to some of the most breathtaking trekking routes and natural attractions in the region. The lush green meadows, dense pine forests, and snow-capped mountains create a tranquil setting that attracts nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. Popular activities include trekking, camping, and river rafting, making Pahalgam a perfect destination for both relaxation and adventure. In addition to its natural beauty, Pahalgam is culturally rich, with several significant sites and festivals. The town is closely associated with the Amarnath Yatra, an annual pilgrimage to the Amarnath Cave, which draws thousands of devotees. Visitors can explore the nearby Betaab Valley, famous for its stunning vistas and Bollywood film shoots, or visit Aru Valley, known for its enchanting meadows and picturesque views. With its blend of adventure, spirituality, and breathtaking scenery, Pahalgam is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring the wonders of Jammu and Kashmir.
