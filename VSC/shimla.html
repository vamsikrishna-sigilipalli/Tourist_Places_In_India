About shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is one of India's most famous hill stations. Known for its scenic beauty, colonial architecture, and pleasant weather, Shimla attracts visitors year-round. The Mall Road is the main hub for shopping, dining, and entertainment, while the Ridge offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. The Viceregal Lodge, now the Indian Institute of Advanced Study, is a stunning example of British colonial architecture and holds historical significance. Additionally, Shimla offers access to outdoor activities like trekking and skiing in nearby Kufri. Jakhoo Temple, located on Jakhoo Hill, is dedicated to Lord Hanuman and provides a serene spiritual experience along with breathtaking views. The lush green valleys, tall pine trees, and snow-capped peaks make Shimla an ideal retreat for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.
